Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

👋 Introduction

Seal Plus respects your privacy and is committed to protecting user data. This Privacy Policy explains how the application handles information when you use Seal Plus.

🔒 Information Collection

Seal Plus does not collect personal information such as name, email, location, or identity data.

  • No account registration required
  • No personal data collection
  • No tracking of user activity

⚙️ How Seal Plus Works

Seal Plus processes media links locally on your device. Downloads and processing activities occur directly on your device without transferring personal data to external servers controlled by the developer.

📱 Permissions Usage

  • Storage Permission — Save downloaded media files to your device
  • Network Access — Download requested content from the internet
  • Notification Permission — Show download progress and completion alerts

🌍 Third-Party Services

Seal Plus may interact with external websites only when users provide links manually. The developer does not control third-party platform privacy policies. Users are responsible for reviewing the privacy practices of any third-party services they interact with.

🛡️ Data Security

All downloads and processing occur locally on the user's device. Seal Plus does not store user information on remote servers. No analytics, no crash reporting services, no telemetry.

👧 Children's Privacy

Seal Plus does not knowingly collect data from children under 13 years of age. The application is designed for general use without any age-specific data practices.

🔄 Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in the application or applicable regulations. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
